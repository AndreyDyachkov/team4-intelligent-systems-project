Course Work of Postgraduate Programme Intelligent Systems

Lung diseases need a quick diagnosis, often in the first hours, which is very important. For medical professionals, detecting lung disease remains a struggle. Causes of disease are often unknown in the first place or how to treat them entirely. If it is discovered early enough, it will be treated easily.

Our purpose is to help radiologists, who, in conditions of a large flow of patients, can reduce the time for interpreting the results of X-ray images, thereby increasing the number of patients accepted.

We use methods of image processing, including noise reduction, feature extraction, and damaged region identification, which help identify parts of the lung afflicted by pneumonia. And with cross-referencing with medical history data, doctors can make more precise decisions. This study demonstrates the use of machine learning and image processing to accurately classify and forecast lung diseases.
